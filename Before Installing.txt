Before installing or using the program,
open the file "environment" inside the
steamRPC folder and change the value
"YOUR_STEAM_ID" to the ID of your Steam
account(To get your ID, go to your Steam
Client, click your photo and choose "Account
Details", your ID will appear below the Title).

And also change the "STEAM_API_KEY"
to the key you get from this site:
https://steamcommunity.com/dev/apikey

(When changing the values, don't remove the "")
